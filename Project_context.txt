## 2️⃣ What exactly your project is

You are building a **Placement Portal Application (PPA)** similar in **flow** (not UI) to:

* Internshala
* Indeed
* AngelList

The portal allows:

* Companies to create placement drives
* Students to apply
* Admin to approve and manage everything

This is **NOT** a production system.
It is an **academic, functional prototype**.

❌ No manual DB creation
